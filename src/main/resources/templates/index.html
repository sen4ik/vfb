About

Long time ago, when I was a youth pastor, I used to send one Bible verse each morning to all the youth members. Youth liked it. It was often a great starter for more messaging between me and youth members. Today I decided to automate the process and that is what this website is about.

If you live in USA or Canada and wish to receive daily inspirational verses from the Bible to your phone, please use the Sign Up form below to subscribe.
